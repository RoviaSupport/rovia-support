To determine the correct login for the transfer link:

  1. Locate the form link on the page of the DreamTrip in question. (You can email this link to any member who needs assistance).
  2. Hover your cursor over the link and in your browser's status bar you will see the URL. We can tell from the below example that this URL corresponds with plane@rovia.com.
  3. Right click on the link to copy the URL and then past it into the address bar of your browser.
  4. Remove everything after '.com' and then proceed to the URL. Now you are ready to log in with the corresponding credentials.






Locating the Flight Manifest

  1. Log into Formstack using the above process.
  2. Enter the DreamTrip number.'in the search field under the Dashboard' tab.
    Note: If you are unable to find a flight submission in one account, please check the other each profile to verify whether or not a submission has been made.
  3. Once you've located the correct form, click 'Submissions' to see the list of entries on the transfer manifest.
    Note: From this screen, you can also generate a link to the form which can be emailed to the member (in case you forgot to do it earlier!).

  4. You will see the transfer manifest, listed by Date Submitted, Dember ID, Order ID, Email and Phone Number.
  5. On this screen you are able to resend notifications to members, or edit the entry*.

    *IMPORTANT DO NOT make any edits to an entry after the transfer deadline, as it will not be received by the transfer company!!!






Troubleshooting

Reactivating an Expired Airport Transfer Form

If a transfer form is not set up correctly in Formstack, the link will expire prematurely. In this case you will need re-activate an expired airport transfer form*.

Note: Use this process ONLYif the deadline has not yet passed. If the deadline has passed, members may arrange their own transportation and submit the receipt for compensation in the form of DT Points.
Once the issue is identified:
  1. Check the deadline listed under the 'What's Included' tab on Dreamtrips.com, or in the confirmation email showing in Fortius. Proceed ONLY if the deadline has not yet passed.
  2. Log into Formstack and locate the correct form. Click under 'Settings,' locate the field labled 'Disable Form' and change the Date and Time to two days after the actual deadline (for time zone purposes). (ie, if a deadline is on July 28, the Disable time should be July 30 12am).
  3. The form will update automatically and the link will remain the same. You can use the same URL link to send to members.
  4. Notate Fortius stating that the form has been updated to reflect the correct date. Also note that airport transfer information as manually been submitted (if applicable).

Creating a Formstack Link*

Note: This process is is
  • For Supervisors only
  • For after DreamTrips business hours only
  • Only if there is no Formstack link available in any of the 6 Formstack profiles.
  1. Log into Formstack.
  2. Search by the DreamTrip number (to ensure you are not duplicating the link).
  3. If nothing is found, type 'Master' into the search box.
  4. Click the checkbox on the right to select the applicable template.
  5. Click 'Copy' and select 'My Account.'

  6. Search for 'Copy' to locate and select the newly created form. Select 'Build.'
  7. Update the title form with format: Trip Number/Destination City/Number corresponding with each login.
    • Example: 1701080_miami(6) for a form under the profile for ncarr@rovia.com.
  8. Check for the transfer deadline (from What's Included or the confirmation email).
  9. This date will be added to the Disable form box (add two additional days for time zone purposes) This date will determine when the link will no longer be accesssible to members.
  10. The message box will also need to be updated with the correct date listed on dreamtrips.com as the final submission date
  11. Update the confirmation email, choose the Emails & Redirects Tab at the top left corner of the webpage.
  12. Review the confirmation email by clicking on the 'Send Test Email' line
  13. Click 'save' to record changes
  14. Click 'Publish' for the URL link
  15. Click on the link in order to preview the page and ensure there are no corrections which need to be made.

Adding a Formstack Link to DreamTrips.com*

Note: This process is is
  • For Supervisors only
  • For after DreamTrips business hours only
  • Only if there is no Formstack link available in any of the 6 Formstack profiles.
  1. Log into Fortius Admin.
  2. Click 'Products' followed by 'Packages'
  3. Enter the DT # in the search box, click enter, and select the trip from the list.
  4. Click 'Content Details'
  5. This will produce the Description (Short, detail & what's included) text boxes. Locate the "Click Here" (There are multiple location where it may be found, i.e. What's Included, Platinum Inclusions, or Transportation) SCROLL DOWN
  6. Click on the Edit Icon (Notepad with a pencil)
  7. Locate the words "Click Here" and highlight with your cursor
  8. Click on the Hyper Link Icon from the tool bar (Globe with chain link in tact not broken)
  9. Return to Formstack, Copy the URL
  10. Return to Fortius and paste the link into the URL Box
  11. Select New Window from the drop down box from the Target Box
  12. Click 'OK.'
  13. Click 'Save.'
